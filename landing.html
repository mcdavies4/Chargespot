How it works

From home to charged
in 3 steps

1
Find your charger
Open ChargeSpot and see every available charging point near you in real time. Filter by speed, network, or price. GPS finds the nearest one instantly.
2
Book & pay in 30 seconds
Select your time slot, enter your vehicle reg, and pay via Stripe. £1.50 reserves your spot. £0.45/kWh for pay-as-you-go charging sessions.
3
Arrive and charge
Show your booking reference at the charger. Your slot is waiting. No hunting, no stress, no range anxiety. Just plug in and go.

🚧 Where We Are & Where We're Going

Honest about what this is

ChargeSpot is in beta. We're an early-stage product solving a real problem. Here's exactly where we are and what's coming.

✅
Live Now
Honour System Reservations
Book a slot, pay £1.50, and a QR sticker on the charger shows your reservation to other drivers.
🤝
In Progress
Network Partnerships
We're in active talks with UK charging networks to integrate ChargeSpot at the hardware level.
🔜
Coming Soon
Hardware Enforcement
Once integrated, your booked slot will be physically locked for you. No one else can use it.
Why book now if it's not enforced yet?
Every booking you make helps us prove demand to charging networks. Real bookings = real leverage in partnership conversations. You're not just a user — you're helping build the product.

And if your slot is ever occupied on arrival — we refund you in full. No questions asked.
